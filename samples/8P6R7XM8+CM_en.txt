## Don't Just See Buildings, See Nature

Taiye Pond ruins are now an artificial lake. Summer brings lotus blossoms, autumn golden reeds, winter migratory birds resting here on their southern flight (mallards, gray herons, egrets all present). Tang Dynasty Taiye Pond was formed by channeling water from Longshou Canal. Poets wrote "watching the moon by Taiye Pond's edge," emperors here boating, watching fish, daydreamingâ€”in gaps between power, also an ordinary person.
